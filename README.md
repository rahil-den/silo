# SILO

> *Good frontend deserves to be stored.*

Silo is an open-source archive of exceptional frontend ideas — a place where reusable frontend work is stored until someone needs it.

---

## What is Silo?

Silo is **an open-source archive of exceptional frontend ideas.**

It is not just a component library.

It stores reusable frontend work that developers, designers, and founders can study, copy, remix, and ship.

The name comes from a grain silo — a place where valuable things are preserved until someone needs them.

The philosophy is simple:

> **Everything here is already done.**

---

## The Six Collections

### 🌐 Internet UX Patterns
Recreate proven experiences from successful software.

> Stripe Checkout · Dropbox Referral · Linear Issues · Notion Sharing · Cursor AI · Airbnb Booking · ChatGPT Interface

Study why they work and rebuild them.

---

### 🚀 Startup UI Database
Reverse engineer modern startups. Research YC companies, Product Hunt launches, AI startups, SaaS products, and design tools.

Recreate: Landing Pages · Pricing · Dashboards · Onboarding · Auth · Search · Billing · AI Experiences

Think of it as source code for great startup design.

---

### 🏛️ UI Archeology
Preserve iconic interfaces from internet history.

> Facebook 2004 · Reddit 2008 · Twitter 2010 · Instagram 2011 · Google 1999 · Airbnb 2012

Every recreation includes design insights and modern implementation.

---

### ⚙️ Product Systems
Focus on complete systems rather than isolated UI.

> Referral System · Waitlist · Authentication · Billing Portal · Team Workspace · Notification Center · Search · Analytics · AI Workspace · Calendar

These should feel production-ready.

---

### 🧪 Frontend Lab
Experimental work.

> SVG · GSAP · Framer Motion · CSS · Canvas · Three.js · Scroll animations · Hover interactions · Physics · Creative coding

A playground for frontend exploration.

---

### ✨ Impossible Collection
Recreate experiences that make developers wonder how they were built.

> Apple Dynamic Island · Vision Pro UI · macOS Dock Physics · Arc Browser · Spotify Wrapped · Figma Multiplayer · Nothing OS · Steam Library

This collection exists to inspire and challenge.

## Design Philosophy

Silo should feel like a premium editorial publication, not a SaaS product.

**Keywords:** Calm · Minimal · Editorial · Architectural · Premium · Intentional · Timeless · Confident

**Avoid:** Loud gradients · Generic startup templates · Dashboard aesthetics · Excessive decoration · Heavy skeuomorphism

Typography should carry the design. Whitespace is a feature. Motion should support the experience, never dominate it.

---

## Visual Language

Inspired by: Apple · Linear · Vercel · Stripe · Granola · Branch · shadcn/ui · Rauno · Godly.website · Minimal Gallery

Take inspiration from their craftsmanship, not their layouts.
